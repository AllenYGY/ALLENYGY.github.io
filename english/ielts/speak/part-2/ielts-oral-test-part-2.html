IELTS ORAL TEST Part-2

Describe a daily routine that you enjoy

You should say:

What it is

When and why you started to follow this routine

Whether it is easy to follow this routine

And explain why you enjoy having this routine in your daily life

[1]

Off the top of my head, the daily routine that I enjoy is playing badminton.

And for when I started to do it is about a year ago.

Because a friend invited me to join their badminton group, and I quickly realized how much I enjoyed it.

As for whether it’s easy to keep/ stick to 坚持做/ continue, I would say yes.

Because although my schedule can get busy with classes, projects, and studying, setting aside specific times for badminton helps me manage my time better. Plus, having friends who play with me keeps me motivated to stick to it.

On top of that, the reason why I am a big fan of/ I am addicted to/ I can’t live without this routine is because it provides a perfect escape from the academic pressures of being a computer science student.

When I play badminton, I feel excited and happy, and it helps me relax. It’s a great way to stay physically fit and also serves as a social activity where I can catch up with friends. Overall, this routine not only keeps me active but also improves my mental well-being, making it an essential part of my life.

学习伙伴

  • Describe a person you really enjoy studying/working with
  • Describe someone you know who made a good decision recently

Off the top of my head, the person I’m thinking about is Yunus.

So… we are best friends. We first met during our freshman year at university when we were assigned to the same dorm room.

Overall, I would say Yunus is compatible with everyone
(adj./从句: 照应 “enjoy studying/working with”).

[4]

Speaking of which, we were having this group work for a Database Management System Project , in the second semester of my sophomore year . And he’s the group leader.

Therefore, he assigned me to do the code work.
And I gotta say this was a very wise decision because I have a strong background in coding and have consistently performed well in our computer science courses.

As a result, our group completed the project ahead of schedule and received the highest grade in the class. This success clearly reflected Yunus’s excellent decision-making skills and his ability to leverage the strengths of his team members effectively.

[5]

On top of that, during the teamwork, he ____, which showed that he’s got great leadership. And everyone_(feeling/reaction) working with him.

On top of that, during the teamwork, he consistently communicated clearly and ensured everyone was on the same page, which showed that he’s got great leadership. And everyone felt motivated and appreciated working with him.

[6]

Therefore, I think Yunus is really a good leader and a great friend to have, and I look forward to working with him on future projects.

有趣的老人 – 一件事情=有趣

  1. Describe an interesting old person you have met
  2. Describe a person you met at the party who you enjoyed talking with
  3. Describe a person who you are happy to know

Off the top of my head, the person I’m thinking about is Mr. Wang.
So, Mr. Wang is a family friend, and we met at my grandfather's
birthday party.
Overall, I think Mr. Wang is well-read and fascinating to talk to.

[7]

Speaking of which, at the party, we somehow ended up sitting next to each other during dinner, so we just started talking.

I gotta say, I was so amazed by the talk with him:

We first talked about his incredible knowledge of various board games. He shared fascinating stories about playing chess, Go, Five in a Row, and Chinese chess, detailing some of the most challenging matches he had played and the different strategies he employed.

Then, he shared his experiences about working as a historian. Mr. Wang had spent several years researching and documenting historical events, and his insights into various historical periods were incredibly enlightening.

Also, he mentioned that he often participated in board game tournaments and had won several local championships, which was a testament to his skill and dedication.

[8]

In a word, Mr. Wang is an incredible storyteller and a treasure trove of knowledge.
Mr. Wang stands out for his vibrant curiosity and his ability to engage others with his experiences. While many elderly people I know tend to focus on routine and familiar topics, Mr. Wang continually seeks out new adventures and shares them in a way that captivates and inspires.

迟到/错过某个重要的会议/活动

1. Describe a time when you missed or were late for an important meeting/event

You should say:

  • When it happened
  • What happened
  • Why you missed/were late for it
  • And explain how you felt about this experience

Off the top of my head, I would like to talk about a time I was late for an interview for a peer mentor program in my university.

So… this happened when I was applying to be part of the peer mentor program at my university.

Basically, this interview was important to me because being a peer mentor would allow me to help incoming students transition to university life, provide academic guidance, and foster a supportive community. It was a chance to develop my leadership and communication skills while making a positive impact on others. I would say it was pretty much a big deal.

Speaking of which, just a few days before the interview, I unfortunately contracted COVID-19. I started experiencing symptoms like fever and fatigue, which made it difficult for me to prepare for the interview and focus on the tasks at hand.

So, one thing led to another, and I had to quarantine myself at home to recover and prevent spreading the virus to others. As a result, I was unable to attend the interview in person and ended up being late for the scheduled time.

As a result, I missed the chance to participate in the peer mentor program that year.

Overall, the experience taught me the importance of resilience and adaptability in the face of unexpected challenges. I learned to prioritize my health and find alternative ways to pursue my goals, such as applying for the program again in the future once I had fully recovered.
