Game Trees

  • A Game (State) Tree models the different possible outcomes in a game.
    • Vertices: positions (states) in a game.
    • Edges: legal moves from one position (state) to another.
    • Leaves: Final positions (states) of a game.
  • eg: Tic-Tac-Toe
